Everything you need to know

Common questions

01. Who can apply to MGX Campus Enugu programs?

MGX Campus is open to students, recent graduates from ESUT, UNN, and other institutions, working professionals, and Enugu State Government personnel. Each program has its own entry requirements — our admissions team will guide you to the right track.

02. How long do programs take to complete?

Bootcamps run 8–16 weeks. Fellowship programs run 3–6 months. Government and enterprise cohorts are customised to your institution's timeline. All programs end with an MGX Campus professional certification.

03. Is MGX Campus physically located in Enugu?

Yes. MGX Campus is physically based in Enugu State with hybrid delivery — core modules available online and lab sessions, workshops, and project work conducted in-person at our Enugu facility. Enterprise programs can be delivered on-site at your organisation.

04. Does MGX Campus help with job placement after graduation?

Yes. Graduates access internship and apprenticeship pathways, direct placement with MGX Research partner organisations across Enugu and Nigeria, and referrals through our industry sponsor network. The MGX ecosystem is itself an employment pipeline.

05. Can Enugu State Government agencies sponsor teams?

Absolutely. We partner with state agencies, LGAs, enterprises, and NGOs across Enugu to design custom cohort training programs. Contact our partnerships team to build a program for your organisation or ministry.
