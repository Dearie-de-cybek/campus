Everything you need to know

Common questions

01. Who can apply to MGX Campus programs?

MGX Campus is open to students, recent graduates, working professionals, and government personnel seeking to advance in technology. Each program has its own entry requirements — our admissions team will guide you to the right track.

02. How long do programs take to complete?

Bootcamps run 8–16 weeks. Fellowship programs are 3–6 months. Government and enterprise training cohorts are customised to client timelines. All programs end with a professional certification.

03. Are programs offered online, in-person, or both?

We offer hybrid delivery — core modules online, labs and project work in-person. Enterprise and government programs can be delivered fully on-site at your location.

04. Does MGX Campus help with job placement after graduation?

Yes. Graduates access our internship and apprenticeship pathways, direct placement with MGX Research partner organisations, and referrals to our industry sponsor network. The MGX ecosystem itself is an employment pipeline.

05. Can organisations sponsor or enroll teams?

Absolutely. We work with enterprises, government agencies, and NGOs to design and deliver custom cohort programs. Contact our partnerships team to discuss a training program for your organisation.
